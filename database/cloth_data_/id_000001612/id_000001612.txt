Outerwear acted as a key silhouette for Gucci's AW18 catwalk show with texture also playing an integral role in the label's offering. This softly constructed wool blend chevron tweed coat is cut to a collarless belted silhouette ideal for both off and on-duty days. Crafted in Italy and presented in black and off white, Gucci's knitted coat is accented with washed blue and red grosgrain ribbon trims and features four front patch pockets, long sleeves, a straight hem and a long length.chevron tweed coat
